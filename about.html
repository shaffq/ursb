About Us

UniKL Resources Sdn. Bhd. (URSB) is a company incorporated in Malaysia under the Companies Act 1965, is a wholly owned subsidiary of Universiti Kuala Lumpur (UniKL). URSB operates as the non-academic business and commercial entity of UniKL, leveraging on UniKLs top-notch resources in technical skills, expertise, and facilities, to offer quality and cost-effective services to clients. Whilst delivering quality projects to clients, URSB contributes towards UniKLs mission to shape its graduates with strong technical knowledge and entrepreneurial skills by incorporating students and staff to ongoing projects thus exposing them to the latest industry requirements.
